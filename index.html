About Plate Inn

History
The first Plate Inn opened its doors on July 7th, 1987, in New York. As a diversified operation focusing on the principles of "Beauty, Hygiene, and Legacy," we are a diverse operation that makes each dish from scratch in contemporary built kitchens. Working with native chefs on hand to provide delicious and genuine cuisine that never displeases, we commenced franchising all over the United States in locations including Miami, San Francisco, Boston, and more.
